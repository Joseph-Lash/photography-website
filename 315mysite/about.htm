About Me

My name is Joseph Lash and I am a photographer based in Rochester, Minnesota. I am studying Computer Science with a Minors in Photography at Liberty University. I am passionate about capturing the beauty of people and the world around me. If you are interested in booking a photoshoot or have any questions, feel free to contact me.
